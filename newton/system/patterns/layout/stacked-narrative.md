## Intent

* Long-form storytelling
* Case studies
* Editorial flow

## Characteristics

* Vertical rhythm
* Clear section breaks
* Predictable hierarchy
* Minimal lateral complexity

## Use when

* Explaining a process
* Walking through a story
* Depth > speed

## Avoid when

* You need immediate persuasion
* The page should resolve in one screen
